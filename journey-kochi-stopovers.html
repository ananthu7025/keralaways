Kochi Stopovers

Three slow days in the city of pepper, ink, and salt air.

The Chapter Outline

Three slow days in the port.

Duration3 Days / 2 Nights
Best SeasonYear Round
FocusHistory, Art galleries, Spice

Kochi is a historic trading city where Arab dhows, Portuguese galleons, Dutch merchants, and British planters all left their marks. Its colonial alleys contain Jewish synagogues, spice markets, and old warehouse art galleries.

Our Kochi Stopovers are curated for travelers looking to spend a few slow days exploring this artistic seaport. We guide you to boutique stays in restored 18th-century townhouses, contemporary biennale hubs, and the slowest, most delicious dosa breakfasts you have ever taken.
