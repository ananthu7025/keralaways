Our Belief

Memories in the connections, not the miles.

At Keralaway Tours, we believe the most memorable journeys are not measured by the number of places visited, but by the stories experienced and the connections made along the way.

Rooted in Kerala and guided by local expertise, we create private travel experiences that combine authentic culture, breathtaking landscapes, meaningful encounters, and personalized service.

From the mist-covered tea plantations of Munnar and the tranquil backwaters of Alleppey to the vibrant traditions of Malabar and the heritage streets of Kochi, every journey is thoughtfully designed around the interests, pace, and preferences of each traveler.

The Origin

Born in the mist, built in the salt air.

— George K., Founder

Keralaway began in the high slopes of Munnar, where the air was cardamom and the hills was mist. George, a third-generation planter, realized that travelers were speeding through his home in air-conditioned buses, checking items off list boxes, and returning home with photos but no stories.

In 2018, he left the plantation and moved to an old spice warehouse on Princess Street in Fort Kochi. He brought together writer friends, local hosts, and old-school sailors to build a slow travel design house. Today, we are nine designers, writers, and logistic navigators operating from that same sunlit warehouse.

We do not build mass itineraries. We curate quiet afternoons. We match you with families who feed you, boat captains who guide you through hidden streams, and Vaidyas who recommend herbs according to the monsoon rains.
